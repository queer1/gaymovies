Site Privacy Statement

In order to rate movies, see your friend's ratings and share your own ratings with friends, you must login via your Facebook account.

This website will ONLY have access to the information that is ALREADY PUBLIC on your facebook page. Here is the specific information that we use from facebook:

  • Your unique Facebook UserId is stored internally to identify you.
  • Your Firstname and Lastname are stored so that they can be displayed to your friends.
  • Your friend's list is accessed (but not saved) to show you a list of friends which also have accounts on here so that you can view their ratings.

In particular, we do NOT access your email addresses, home address, or phone numbers, nor will we share ANY information with any third party. Furthermore, any attempt by this website to access any of your private information IN THE FUTURE, would result in an authorization dialog box being displayed. (We aren't planning to do this anyway, but the point is that Facebook would inform you on-login even if we did).

If you don't trust us, Facebook, or the above statements in general and are concerned about your privacy, you can be given a "standard" (non-facebook) account upon request (see contact info below). With a standard account, you could rate movies but you couldn't share the ratings or see your friend's ratings.

This website gathers your movie ratings and comments for specific movies that you choose. This informaion may be shared with others on the site including but not limited to your facebook friends. In addition, the information will be used to determine average ratings, movie recommendations, and other unspecified derivative information.

If you have any questions or concerns, you may contact us at gaymovielist@gmail.com.
